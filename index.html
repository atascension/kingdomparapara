A word from Sharique

God first had to unlock the treasure in my heart in order for me to step into Kingdom impact.

As a registered nurse, I walked into rooms that held more than what any chart could capture. I cared for patients whose bodies were sick but whose spirits were crying out for something medicine could not prescribe. God would download things to me before I ever walked through the door. Sometimes in a dream the night before. Sometimes while reviewing a chart in the morning. Sometimes the moment I crossed the threshold of a room, I would feel His movement and I would move with Him.

I knew what I was carrying. What I did not know was that where I was serving was beginning to conflict with what God was trying to birth out of me.

He showed me in a dream that I was servicing an altar that was not His with a particular nursing situation. He told me plainly that my job was now interfering with my intercession.

I did not want to hear it. I had finally found my footing as a nurse, finally learned how to walk in the grace of God on that floor. Through this instruction, my grace was shifting outside of the hospital. That is exactly when He said it was time to go.

I couldn't deny His conviction. The confirmation through dreams, secret places, and words from the Lord.

What I know now is that God will interrupt your comfort to protect His call through your life. He will close the door you have been gripping because He can see what is waiting on the other side of your yes.

In this season of my life, The Lord is downloading into my heart and mind, His wisdom to help cure diseases and showing me that it all leads back to the heart.

Out of the rubble and obedience to God, was Kingdom Para Para made new.
